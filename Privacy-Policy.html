Privacy Policy

Invoice Generator for Freelancers

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Overview

Invoice Generator for Freelancers (“the App”) is designed to help users create, manage, and share invoices directly from their device.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This app operates with a local-only data model, meaning your data remains on your device.

2. Data Handling

2.1 Data Entered by Users

The app allows users to manually enter information such as:

  • Client names
  • Contact details (email, phone number)
  • Invoice details (items, prices, totals, notes)

This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Not transmitted to any external servers
  • Not accessible by the developer

2.2 Data Collection

  • Does not collect personal data to any server
  • Does not track users
  • Does not use analytics or monitoring tools

2.3 Data Sharing

The App does not share user data with third parties.

When you choose to share an invoice (via email or messaging apps), this is a user-initiated action using your device’s built-in sharing system. We do not control or track this activity.

3. Data Storage

All data created within the App is stored locally, including:

  • Client information
  • Invoice records
  • Generated PDF files

Data is stored in the app’s private storage directory. Other apps cannot access this data without your action. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

4. Permissions

The App is designed to function with minimal or no permissions.

  • No access to location
  • No access to contacts
  • No access to camera
  • No unnecessary storage permissions
  • No internet access (if applicable)

5. Third-Party Services

  • No third-party services
  • No ads or SDKs
  • No external API integrations

6. Data Security

The app relies on Android’s built-in security features such as sandboxing and private storage. Since data is not transmitted over the internet, risks related to interception are minimized.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
