Privacy Policy

Invoice Generator for Freelancers

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

🎉 Privacy First! This app is designed so that your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

1. Overview

Invoice Generator helps freelancers create and manage invoices easily. We respect your privacy and ensure that your data remains under your control.

2. Data Handling

2.1 Data Entered by Users

The app allows you to enter information such as:

  • Client names
  • Contact details (email, phone number)
  • Invoice details (items, pricing, totals, notes)

This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Not transmitted to any external servers
  • Not accessible by the developer

2.2 Data Collection

The app does not collect or transmit personal data to any external servers. All data you enter remains on your device.

2.3 Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

When you share an invoice (via email or messaging apps), this is a user-initiated action using your device’s built-in sharing system. We do not track or store this activity.

3. Data Storage

  • Client information is stored locally on your device
  • Invoices are stored locally in the app database
  • PDF files are generated and saved in app-specific storage

All data remains on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

4. Permissions

This app is designed to work with minimal or no permissions.

  • No location access
  • No contacts access
  • No camera access
  • No unnecessary storage permissions
  • No internet access (if applicable)

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use:

  • Third-party services
  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • External APIs

6. Data Security

Your data is protected using Android’s built-in security mechanisms such as app sandboxing and private storage.

Since the app does not transmit data over the internet, risks related to data interception are minimized.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
