📋 Overview

Invoice Generator is designed with your privacy as the top priority. Unlike most apps, we follow a "Zero Data Collection" policy. This means:

  • ✅ No accounts or sign-ups required - Just download and use
  • ✅ No data collection - We don't collect anything
  • ✅ No tracking - No analytics, no cookies, no trackers
  • ✅ No internet required - Works completely offline
  • ✅ No permissions needed - Zero permissions in manifest

🔒 Data Storage

All data created in Invoice Generator is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Client Information: Stored in local database (Room) - never leaves your device
  • Invoices: Stored in local database - never leaves your device
  • PDF Files: Generated and saved in your app's private storage - only accessible by this app

When you share an invoice via email or messaging apps, the PDF is shared through Android's built-in sharing system. This is a one-time transfer and we don't store or track these shares.

📱 Permissions

This app requires ZERO permissions. Here's the complete list (it's empty!):

No Permissions The app works completely without any special permissions

For technical accuracy, here's what we don't use:

  • ❌ Internet - The app works completely offline
  • ❌ Storage - Uses scoped storage (Android's built-in file system) which needs no permission
  • ❌ Camera - Not needed for invoice generation
  • ❌ Contacts - You type client details manually
  • ❌ Location - No reason to know your location

📄 PDF Files and Sharing

When you generate and share PDF invoices:

  • PDFs are saved in your device's app-specific storage (Android/data/com.faheem.invoicegenerator/files/)
  • These files are only accessible by this app
  • When you share via email/messaging apps, Android creates a temporary copy for that app
  • We have no access to where you share or who you share with
  • If you uninstall the app, all PDFs and data are automatically deleted

🔐 Data Security

  • Local Only: All data stays on your device - no servers, no cloud
  • No Transmission: Since there's no internet, data cannot be intercepted
  • App Isolation: Android's sandboxing prevents other apps from accessing your invoice data
  • Uninstall = Delete: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data

👤 Third-Party Services

None. This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks. There are no trackers, no cookies, and no background data collection.

📤 Data Sharing

We share nothing. Since we collect no data, there's nothing to share. When you voluntarily share PDF invoices through email or other apps, that's a conscious action you take - we don't facilitate or track it.

🌍 International Users
